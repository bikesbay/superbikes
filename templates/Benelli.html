Overview

The Benelli TRK 502 is a versatile adventure motorcycle from Italy, designed for both on-road touring and off-road exploration. Powered by a 499cc parallel-twin, liquid-cooled engine producing around 47 horsepower, it delivers smooth and manageable power suitable for long rides and varied terrains. The bike features modern electronics including ABS and ride-by-wire throttle for enhanced safety and control. Its rugged chassis, long-travel suspension, and dual-purpose tires ensure stability and comfort over different surfaces, while the ergonomic design and spacious seating make it ideal for both rider and passenger. With its combination of practicality, reliability, and adventure-ready capabilities, the TRK 502 is an excellent choice for riders seeking a comfortable and capable all-rounder.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Benelli TRK 502 retains its rugged and adventure-ready design, making it stand out on both highways and off-road trails. The paint and bodywork still maintain much of their original finish, highlighting the bike’s robust character and practical styling. As with most used motorcycles, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the body panels, frame, and protective elements remain in good condition, making this TRK 502 an appealing choice for riders seeking a capable and versatile adventure bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

The bike comes with a digital instrument cluster, including a Bluetooth-enabled 5-inch TFT display on the 2025 model. LED lighting ensures visibility, while a tall windscreen, knuckle guards, and a comfortable seat enhance comfort for both rider and passenger. A substantial 20-liter fuel tank allows for extended touring range, and a Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) adds an extra layer of safety.

Engine & Performance

The Benelli TRK 502 is powered by a 500cc, liquid-cooled, parallel-twin engine with DOHC, producing approximately 46.9 bhp (47.5 PS) at 8,500 rpm and 46 Nm of peak torque at 6,000 rpm. The engine is paired with a smooth 6-speed gearbox and utilizes Bosch electronic fuel injection (EFI) for efficient power delivery, making it suitable for both city rides and long-distance touring.

Chassis and Handling

The TRK 502 features a sturdy steel trellis frame for stability and agility. Front suspension consists of 50mm upside-down (USD) telescopic forks with adjustable preload and rebound, while the rear is equipped with a hydraulic monoshock absorber with adjustment. Braking is handled by dual 320mm front discs and a 260mm rear disc, supported by a dual-channel ABS system that can be switched off for off-road use, ensuring strong stopping power and rider confidence.

Variants

The TRK 502 is available in two versions: the standard TRK 502 with 17-inch alloy wheels, and the adventure-focused TRK 502X, which features 19-inch front and 17-inch rear cross-spoke wheels designed for enhanced off-road capability.
