Overview

The Royal Enfield Himalayan is a rugged adventure motorcycle from India, designed for both on-road touring and off-road exploration. Powered by a 411cc single-cylinder, air-cooled engine producing around 24.3 horsepower, it delivers manageable power suitable for long rides and challenging terrain. The bike features modern electronics including dual-channel ABS for safety and precise control. Its sturdy frame, long-travel suspension, and dual-purpose tires provide stability and comfort across diverse surfaces, while the upright ergonomics and spacious seating ensure rider and passenger comfort. With its combination of durability, practicality, and adventure-ready capability, the Himalayan is an ideal choice for riders seeking a reliable and versatile adventure motorcycle.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Royal Enfield Himalayan retains its rugged and utilitarian design, built to handle both city roads and off-road trails. The paint and body panels still maintain much of their original finish, highlighting the bike’s robust character and adventure-ready styling. As with most used motorcycles, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the frame, bodywork, and protective elements remain in good condition, making this Himalayan a reliable choice for riders seeking a durable adventure bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Chassis & Suspension

The bike features upside-down front forks that provide excellent control and stability across varied terrain, while a linkage-type monoshock rear suspension ensures a smooth and balanced ride. With a substantial ground clearance of 230 mm and a robust twin-spar tubular frame, the Himalayan is built to handle rugged paths and challenging adventures with confidence.

Engine & Performance

The Royal Enfield Himalayan is powered by a modern, liquid-cooled 452cc single-cylinder engine in the latest Himalayan 450 model, delivering strong and reliable performance. Older models feature a 411cc air-cooled engine paired with a 5-speed transmission. The Himalayan 450 also includes multiple riding modes, allowing riders to adapt power delivery to different conditions, making it suitable for both city riding and challenging off-road terrain.

Braking & Wheels

Durable spoke wheels are designed for adventure riding, with newer models compatible with tubeless tyres for enhanced reliability. Large wheel sizes—21-inch at the front and 17-inch at the rear—provide superior off-road capability and stability over rough surfaces.

Technology & Features

The Himalayan is equipped with a digital instrument cluster that displays essential riding information, along with a Tripper Navigation system featuring a circular screen that provides turn-by-turn guidance via Bluetooth connection to the Royal Enfield app. An assist and slipper clutch improves gear shifting smoothness and reduces rider effort, adding to the bike’s overall practicality and comfort.
