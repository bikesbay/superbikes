Overview

The Triumph Tiger 900 is a versatile adventure motorcycle from the UK, designed to excel on both highways and rugged off-road trails. Powered by an 888cc inline-three engine producing around 94 horsepower, it delivers smooth, responsive performance suitable for long-distance touring and technical terrain. The bike features advanced electronics, including multiple riding modes, traction control, and cornering ABS, ensuring safety and precise handling in diverse conditions. Its lightweight chassis, long-travel suspension, and ergonomic design provide stability and comfort for both rider and passenger. With its blend of power, technology, and adventure-ready capability, the Tiger 900 is a top choice for riders seeking a reliable and versatile all-rounder for touring and off-road adventures.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Triumph Tiger 900 retains its rugged and adventure-ready design, built to handle both highways and off-road trails. The paintwork and body panels still maintain much of their original finish, highlighting the bike’s robust character and practical styling. As with most used adventure motorcycles, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the frame, fairings, and protective elements remain in good condition, making this Tiger 900 a reliable choice for riders seeking a durable adventure bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

Both GT and Rally Pro models feature a full-color 7-inch TFT display with the "My Triumph Connectivity System" for navigation, music, and calls. Riding modes include Road, Rain, Sport, and Off-Road, with the Rally Pro adding Off-Road Pro and a Rider-configurable mode. Optimized cornering ABS and advanced traction control provide enhanced stability and grip across various conditions.

Engine & Performance

The Triumph Tiger 900 is powered by an 888cc liquid-cooled, in-line 3-cylinder engine with a T-plane crank, delivering 108 PS at 9,500 rpm and 90 Nm of torque at 6,850 rpm. It features a 6-speed gearbox with a wet, multi-plate, slip-and-assist clutch, and the Rally Pro/GT Pro variants include a bi-directional quickshifter. The 20-liter fuel tank ensures long-range touring capability.

Chassis and Brakes

The Tiger 900 is built on a tubular steel trellis frame with a bolt-on aluminum subframe. Suspension and wheel specifications vary between the GT and Rally Pro models to suit their respective purposes, offering optimized handling and comfort for both touring and off-road adventure riding.

Other Features

Cruise control, all-LED lighting with signature DRL, heated grips (standard on Pro models), heated seats (Pro variants), and a USB power socket complete the Tiger 900’s touring-friendly setup.
