Overview

The KTM RC8 is a pioneering Austrian superbike that broke new ground in the liter-class segment. Powered by a 1,195cc 75° V-twin engine, it delivers approximately 135 horsepower and 120 Nm of torque, propelling the bike to speeds over 270 km/h. The RC8 features a lightweight steel trellis frame and high-performance WP suspension, offering exceptional agility and stability. Its aggressive design, highlighted by sharp lines and distinctive orange accents, reflects KTM's racing DNA. With its blend of power, precision, and bold aesthetics, the RC8 stands as a testament to KTM's commitment to innovation in the superbike arena.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned KTM RC8e retains its aggressive, race-inspired styling that makes it stand out on both the road and track. The sharp, angular fairings and signature KTM orange accents still maintain much of their original glossy finish, emphasizing the bike’s sporty character. As with most used superbikes, there are minor scratches on the bodywork from regular riding, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the body panels and frame remain in good condition, making this RC8e an appealing choice for riders seeking a high-performance sport bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics & Aerodynamics

The bike is equipped with a 5-inch TFT display featuring an AIM MXS 1.2 RACE data logger and built-in GPS for professional-level telemetry. Rider aids include adjustable traction control, anti-wheelie, engine brake control, and Quick shifter+ for seamless gear changes. Its aerodynamic design incorporates MotoGP-inspired winglets that generate downforce and improve high-speed stability, ensuring the RC8e combines cutting-edge technology with extreme track performance.

Engine & Performance

The KTM RC8e is powered by an 889cc LC8c parallel-twin engine, derived from the KTM 890 Duke R but specifically tuned for track performance. It produces 135 horsepower at 11,000 rpm and 98 Nm of torque at 8,250 rpm. With a dry weight of just 142 kg, partly thanks to carbon Kevlar-reinforced bodywork, the bike offers an exceptional power-to-weight ratio. A full titanium Abramovic racing exhaust further enhances performance and adds a signature race-ready sound.

Chassis and Handling

The RC8e features a bespoke 25CrMo4 tubular steel frame for rigidity and precise handling. Its race-developed WP Apex Pro suspension includes a fully adjustable 43mm closed-cartridge front fork and a fully adjustable rear monoshock. Braking duties are handled by a race-spec Brembo system with Stylemark front calipers on 290mm discs, while ultra-light forged aluminum Dymag wheels reduce unsprang weight for sharper handling.

Ergonomics

The KTM RC8e features a steel trellis frame optimized for dynamic performance and precise handling. Its ergonomics are carefully designed, with refined contact points on the fuel tank that enhance rider connection and adjustable foot pegs to suit different riding styles. The bike’s suspension includes a fully adjustable WP Apex Open Cartridge front fork and an adjustable rear monoshock, allowing riders to fine-tune handling for both track and road conditions. Braking is handled by a high-performance Brembo system with Hypure calipers, providing confidence-inspiring stopping power under aggressive riding.
