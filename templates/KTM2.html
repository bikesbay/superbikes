Overview

The KTM 390 Adventure is a compact adventure motorcycle designed for riders seeking agility, versatility, and off-road capability. Powered by a 373cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine producing around 44 horsepower, it delivers responsive and manageable performance for both city streets and light off-road trails. The bike features advanced electronics such as ride-by-wire throttle, multiple riding modes, and switchable ABS to enhance safety and control. Its lightweight chassis, long-travel suspension, and upright ergonomics provide stability, comfort, and confidence over varied terrain. With its combination of technology, performance, and adventure-ready design, the KTM 390 Adventure is an ideal choice for riders looking for a nimble and capable all-rounder for touring and off-road exploration.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned KTM 390 Adventure retains its rugged and purposeful adventure design, built to perform on both city streets and light off-road trails. The paint and body panels still maintain much of their original finish, highlighting the bike’s sporty and durable character. As with most used motorcycles, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the frame, fairings, and protective elements remain in good condition, making this 390 Adventure a reliable choice for riders seeking a nimble and capable adventure bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

The KTM 390 Adventure comes equipped with advanced electronics to enhance rider control and safety. Cornering MTC (Motorcycle Traction Control) ensures optimal traction even when leaned, while Cornering ABS and Off-Road ABS provide superior braking confidence across surfaces. Riders can choose from three ride modes—Street, Rain, and Off-Road—offering different throttle maps and ABS calibrations. The bike also features Quickshifter+ for clutchless up and down shifts, cruise control for long rides, and a 5-inch TFT display that provides comprehensive information and connectivity.

WP Apex Suspension

The long-travel WP Apex suspension offers 200mm of front travel and 205mm at the rear, ensuring comfort and control on rough terrain. Adjustable preload and rebound on the shock absorber allow riders to tailor the suspension to various riding conditions, providing stability and a smooth ride.

Ergonomics and Chassis

The bike’s rally-inspired design provides agility for both standing and seated riding positions. Increased ground clearance enhances protection and handling on varied surfaces, while an aluminum bash plate adds extra protection for critical components during off-road adventures.

Durable Wheels & Tires

The KTM 390 Adventure is fitted with 21-inch front and 17-inch rear spoke wheels, designed for durability and off-road capability on dirt trails. Tubeless tires add convenience and reliability for both on- and off-road riding.
