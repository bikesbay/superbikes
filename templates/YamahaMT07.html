Overview

The Yamaha MT-07 is a popular middleweight naked motorcycle from Japan, celebrated for its versatility and engaging performance. It is powered by a 689cc parallel-twin, liquid-cooled engine producing around 73 horsepower, offering a strong balance of torque and smooth acceleration. With a top speed of about 210 km/h, the MT-07 delivers plenty of excitement for both city riding and highway cruising. Its lightweight frame, agile handling, and responsive suspension make it approachable for new riders while still thrilling for experienced ones. Equipped with ABS, refined ergonomics, and a minimalist yet aggressive design, the MT-07 combines everyday practicality with sporty character, making it one of the most well-rounded and accessible bikes in its class.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Yamaha MT-07 retains its bold, naked-street styling that gives it a distinctive presence on both city streets and open roads. The paint still holds much of its glossy finish, emphasizing the bike’s muscular tank and minimalistic, aggressive design. As with most used motorcycles, there are minor scratches on the sides from regular riding, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect the bike’s performance or handling. Overall, the body panels and frame remain in good condition, making this MT-07 an appealing choice for riders seeking a stylish, versatile bike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

This bike comes with a compact LED projector headlight that delivers bright illumination and a distinctive “MT” signature look. A fully digital console displays essential riding information clearly, and dual-channel ABS enhances safety by preventing wheel lock-up under hard braking. At the rear, the LED tail light improves visibility and adds to the bike’s modern styling.

Engine & Performance

The Yamaha MT-07 is powered by a 689cc liquid-cooled, DOHC, 4-stroke, 4-valve parallel-twin engine with a 270° crossplane crankshaft, known as the CP2 engine. It comes with a standard 6-speed gearbox for flexibility across various riding conditions, while newer variants offer an optional automatic manual transmission (Y-AMT) that can be toggled on or off for seamless gear changes. An assist and slipper clutch reduces clutch lever effort and provides greater stability during aggressive downshifts, enhancing both comfort and control.

Chassis and Handling

The MT-07 features a lightweight and rigid diamond frame that contributes to its agile handling. The front suspension uses conventional telescopic forks for responsive steering, while the rear employs a link-type swingarm that supports the rear wheel and ensures smooth damping over varying road conditions.

Key Specifications

The MT-07 has an engine capacity of 689cc, delivering a maximum power of 54.0 kW (73.4 PS) at 8,750 rpm and a peak torque of 67 Nm at 6,500 rpm. The bike has a kerb weight of 184 kg, combining lightness with stability for an engaging riding experience.
