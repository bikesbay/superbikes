Overview

The Suzuki Hayabusa is a legendary Japanese superbike, renowned for its combination of high-speed performance and smooth touring capability. Powered by a 1,340cc inline-four engine producing around 197 horsepower, it can reach top speeds of approximately 312 km/h, making it one of the fastest production motorcycles in the world. The bike features advanced electronics, including traction control, launch control, and multiple riding modes, ensuring both safety and optimal performance. Its aerodynamic bodywork, lightweight chassis, and robust braking system provide stability and precise handling at extreme speeds. With its blend of power, comfort, and cutting-edge technology, the Hayabusa is a top choice for riders seeking a high-performance motorcycle that excels both on the track and on long-distance rides.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Suzuki Hayabusa retains its sleek, aerodynamic design that has made it an icon on both highways and tracks. The glossy paintwork still highlights the bike’s smooth, flowing lines and muscular presence. As with most used motorcycles, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the body panels and frame remain in good condition, making this Hayabusa an appealing choice for riders seeking a high-performance superbike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics & Rider Aids (S.I.R.S.)

The Hayabusa incorporates advanced electronics for enhanced control and safety. A six-axis Bosch Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) monitors pitch, roll, and yaw, while the Suzuki Drive Mode Selector Alpha (SDMS Alpha) allows riders to customize engine and control settings. Motion Track Traction Control adjusts power based on lean angle to prevent wheel spin, and a bi-directional quick shift system enables clutchless upshifts and downshifts. Additional aids include launch control with three modes, an active speed limiter, cruise control, and engine brake control with three settings for optimized stability.

Engine & Performance

The Suzuki Hayabusa is powered by a 1,340cc, liquid-cooled, DOHC, inline-four engine that delivers robust power with smooth, responsive delivery. It produces 190 PS of power and 150 Nm of torque, providing exhilarating acceleration. The engine is paired with a 6-speed transmission featuring an assist and slipper clutch, ensuring smooth gear changes under all conditions.

Braking & Wheels

Stopping power comes from Brembo Stylema® front calipers, complemented by a switchable ABS system. The Motion Track Brake System uses IMU data to control brake pressure and prevent wheel lock, while slope-dependent control prevents rear wheel lift during braking on inclines. Hill Hold Control automatically engages the rear brake briefly when stopped on a slope, and the bike runs on exclusively designed Bridgestone tyres for superior grip and performance.

Other Key Features

The Hayabusa features bright and efficient LED headlights and rear combination lights for improved visibility. Its instrument cluster combines analog and digital displays, providing real-time information such as lean angle and acceleration. The aerodynamic bodywork contributes to high-speed stability, while durable engineering, including optimized crankshaft oil passages and reinforced engine case threads, enhances the bike’s longevity and reliability.
