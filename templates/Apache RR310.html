Overview

The TVS Apache RR 310 is a premium sportbike from India, designed to deliver race-inspired performance in an accessible package. Powered by a 312cc single-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine with a reverse-inclined layout, it produces around 34 horsepower and reaches speeds up to 160 km/h. With multiple riding modes, throttle-by-wire, and dual-channel ABS, it balances performance and safety. Its trellis frame, KYB suspension, and aerodynamic fairing ensure sharp handling and stability, while the digital display, lap timer, and Michelin tyres add modern appeal. Combining agility, technology, and style, the RR 310 is an excellent choice for riders seeking an entry-level sportbike that blends thrill with everyday usability.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned TVS Apache RR 310 continues to showcase its sharp, fully-faired design that gives it a premium, race-inspired look. The red-and-black paint scheme still holds much of its shine, emphasizing the bike’s sporty appeal. Like most used sportbikes, there are a few minor scratches on the fairing and side panels from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not impact performance or safety. Overall, the bodywork and panels remain solid, making this RR 310 an attractive option for riders who want a stylish, aerodynamic sportbike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

A 5-inch color TFT display that serves as the instrument cluster and allows easy selection of ride modes. The system supports Bluetooth connectivity, enabling smartphone integration for added convenience. Riders can also use the SmartXonnect app, which offers features such as race analytics, ride pattern analysis, and turn-by-turn navigation. Multiple riding modes—Track, Urban, Rain, and Sport—further enhance the experience by adjusting engine power delivery and electronic settings to suit different conditions.

Engine & Performance

The TVS Apache RR 310 is powered by a 312.2cc, single-cylinder, reverse-inclined, four-valve, liquid-cooled engine that adapts its performance based on the selected riding mode. In Urban and Rain modes, it delivers 25.8 PS, while in Sport and Track modes it produces up to 34 PS on older models and 38 PS on newer ones. Torque output also varies with the mode, offering stronger performance in Sport and Track settings. The engine is paired with a smooth 6-speed gearbox, and an optional bi-directional quickshifter enhances the riding experience by enabling clutchless upshifts and downshifts.

Chassis and Handling

An inverted telescopic fork at the front and a two-arm swingarm at the rear, providing a balance of comfort and sporty handling. For riders seeking more precision, an optional Dynamic Kit is available, which equips the bike with fully adjustable KYB front and rear suspension. Its specially designed sportbike chassis further enhances performance-oriented handling, making the RR 310 stable and agile on both road and track.

Safety & Handling

A range of advanced safety and handling features, especially with the optional Dynamic Pro kit. Cornering ABS enhances braking stability while leaning, and cornering traction control helps prevent rear-wheel slip during turns. Launch control electronically manages throttle input for optimal acceleration from a standstill, while cornering drag torque control balances engine braking to avoid skidding in corners. Additionally, a tyre pressure monitoring system (TPMS) keeps track of tyre health and alerts the rider if pressure falls outside the recommended range, ensuring both performance and safety.
