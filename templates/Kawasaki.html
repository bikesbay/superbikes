Overview

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is an extreme-performance track-focused superbike from Japan, built to push the limits of speed and engineering. Powered by a 998cc supercharged inline-four engine delivering an astonishing 310 horsepower (and up to 326 hp with ram air), it can reach speeds exceeding 400 km/h under ideal conditions. The bike features advanced electronics, including traction control, launch control, and multiple riding modes, ensuring precision and safety at extreme speeds. Its lightweight trellis frame, high-spec Öhlins suspension, and Brembo brakes provide exceptional stability, while its aerodynamic carbon-fiber bodywork with winglets enhances downforce and high-speed control. With its unparalleled combination of raw power, cutting-edge technology, and track-oriented design, the Ninja H2R stands as one of the most extreme and thrilling motorcycles in the world.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Kawasaki Ninja H2R retains its aggressive, aerodynamic design that sets it apart on both track and road. The carbon-fiber bodywork and glossy paint still highlight the bike’s high-performance character and sharp styling. As with most used track-focused superbikes, there are minor scratches and scuffs from regular use, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the body panels and frame remain in excellent condition, making this H2R a compelling choice for riders seeking a race-inspired superbike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Aerodynamics & Design

The H2R’s design is fully track-focused, incorporating integrated winglets that provide downforce and enhance stability at high speeds. It is a track-only machine, lacking components like headlamps, taillights, rear-view mirrors, and a number plate holder. The self-repairing silver-mirror paintwork adds a unique and advanced finish, emphasizing its high-tech, race-inspired appearance.

Engine & Performance

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is powered by a 998cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, inline four-cylinder engine featuring a proprietary Kawasaki supercharger. It delivers up to 310 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful production motorcycles in the world. The bike uses a specialized dog-ring 6-speed gearbox for faster and smoother gear changes, paired with a hydraulic clutch system that includes a back-torque limiter for efficient operation under extreme conditions.

Electronics & Technology

The bike features advanced electronics including traction control, launch control, and a quick-shifter to optimize performance and rider control. An Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) monitors chassis orientation in real time, while dual-channel ABS and an Öhlins Electronic Steering Damper ensure stability and confidence, particularly at extreme speeds.

Chassis & Suspension

The Ninja H2R uses a steel trellis frame for rigidity and precise handling, paired with a single-sided swingarm. Its suspension consists of a fully adjustable KYB front fork and a rear monoshock integrated with the swingarm, while braking is managed by 330mm front discs and a 250mm rear disc with Brembo radial-mount calipers, delivering exceptional stopping power.
