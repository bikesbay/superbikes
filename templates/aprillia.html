Overview

The Aprilia RSV4 is a flagship superbike from Italy, known for its racing DNA and thrilling performance. Powered by a 1,099cc V4 engine delivering about 217 horsepower, it can reach speeds near 300 km/h. The bike features advanced electronics like traction control, wheelie control, and multiple riding modes, ensuring both power and safety. Its lightweight frame, Brembo brakes, and aerodynamic design with winglets provide exceptional handling and stability. With its blend of speed, agility, and cutting-edge technology, the RSV4 is a top choice for riders who want a race-inspired superbike experience.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Aprilia RSV4 still carries its aggressive and aerodynamic design that makes it stand out on both road and track. The paint retains much of its sharp, glossy finish, highlighting the bike’s sporty character. However, as with most used superbikes, there are visible scratches on both the right and left sides, which come from regular use. These marks are cosmetic and do not affect the bike’s performance or handling. Despite these minor imperfections, the overall body panels remain in good condition, making this RSV4 a strong choice for riders who value performance with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Brakes & Wheels

Braking System: Features a Brembo braking system with 330mm front discs and 220mm rear discs with Brembo Stylema callipers. Wheels & Tires: Equipped with alloy wheels and tubeless tires, typically 17 inches in size

Engine & Performance

Engine: A 1,099cc V4 engine provides exceptional power and torque, making it a very powerful street-legal superbike. Power: Delivers up to 220 hp. Transmission: A six-speed gearbox with a bi-directional quickshifter is standard. Aerodynamics: The bodywork is designed to reduce turbulence and enhance stability at high speeds.

Chassis & Suspension

Frame: A dual-beam aluminum frame provides a strong and lightweight foundation. Suspension: The Ohlins electronic suspension is available with a 43mm USD fork and monoshock, offering adjustable settings for track or road.

Dimensions, & Capacity

The motorcycle has a saddle height of 845 mm, a fuel tank capacity of 17.9–18.5 L (including a 4 L reserve), and a kerb weight of about 202 kg, depending on the trim.
