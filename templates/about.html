BikesBayGroup – Who We Are, What We Do, and What We’ve Done

We founded BikesBay in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision — to make buying and selling premium bikes easier, faster, and more trustworthy. What started as a small team of passionate riders soon evolved into a growing community of bike enthusiasts and sellers across the country.

Within just a few years, we’ve connected hundreds of buyers and sellers, offering services like test rides, verified listings, and free first-time servicing on every purchase. Our success comes from our dedication to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

The BikesBay team is made up of skilled developers, marketing experts, and bike lovers who share one mission — to revolutionize the way people experience pre-owned bikes. The name BikesBay represents a hub — a trusted marketplace where every bike finds its perfect rider. Our journey continues as we strive to make premium bikes accessible to everyone, one smooth ride at a time.
