Overview

The BMW S1000RR is a flagship superbike from Germany, celebrated for its blend of high performance and advanced technology. Powered by a 999cc inline-four engine producing around 206 horsepower, it offers blistering acceleration and can reach top speeds exceeding 300 km/h. The bike is equipped with a sophisticated electronics package, including multiple riding modes, traction control, cornering ABS, launch control, and quickshifter, ensuring a perfect balance of power and rider safety. Its lightweight chassis, premium suspension components, and aerodynamic design with distinctive winglets deliver exceptional handling and stability on both road and track. Combining speed, precision, and cutting-edge features, the S1000RR is a top contender for riders seeking a race-inspired superbike experience.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned BMW S1000RR retains its aggressive and aerodynamic styling that instantly commands attention on both road and track. The bike’s signature sharp lines and bold contours are complemented by a glossy paint finish that still shines brightly, emphasizing its sporty and high-performance nature. As expected with a used superbike, there are minor scratches and scuffs on the fairings and side panels, mainly from regular riding and parking, but these are purely cosmetic and do not compromise the bike’s structural integrity or handling. The iconic winglets and sleek bodywork remain intact, preserving the aerodynamic efficiency for which the S1000RR is known. Overall, the bike’s exterior is in good shape, making it an excellent choice for riders looking for a powerful machine with only subtle signs of use.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Electronics and Technology

Riding Modes: Includes multiple modes such as Rain, Road, Dynamic, and Race, allowing riders to adjust the bike's performance characteristics. ABS Pro: Switchable ABS with specific settings for different riding modes, including a mode for racing slicks. Dynamic Traction Control (DTC): Provides enhanced grip and stability, with track-tuned settings available. Hill Start Control Pro: An automatic feature that helps with starting on inclines. M Sport Components: Option to equip M lightweight battery and M Carbon wheels.

Engine & Performance

Engine: A 999cc, water/oil-cooled, 4-stroke, 4-cylinder inline engine with four titanium valves per cylinder. BMW ShiftCam: Variable valve timing technology that enhances both low and mid-range performance. Transmission: A 6-speed gearbox with a wet multiplate clutch, including a bidirectional quick shifter. Power: Delivers high horsepower and torque, with a maximum output of over 206 bhp at high RPM.

Chassis and Handling

Chassis: A bridge-type frame for rigidity and responsiveness. Suspension: Fully adjustable suspension system, including upside-down telescopic forks at the front and an aluminum swingarm at the rear. Aerodynamic Winglets: Aircraft-inspired winglets integrated into the fairing to generate downforce at high speeds, improving stability.

Other Key Features

TFT Display: A high-resolution TFT instrument panel that provides a wealth of information and is easy to read. Bluetooth Connectivity: Allows for mobile phone integration for navigation and other features. Heated Grips: For enhanced comfort during cooler riding conditions. Brake System: Features twin disc brakes with four-piston radial calipers at the front and a single-piston caliper at the rear.
