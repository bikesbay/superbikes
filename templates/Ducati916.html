Overview

The Ducati 916 is an iconic Italian superbike, celebrated for its racing heritage and striking design. Powered by a 916cc V-twin engine producing around 114 horsepower, it offers exhilarating performance and a top speed of approximately 245 km/h. The bike combines advanced engineering with rider-focused features, including a lightweight trellis frame, sophisticated suspension, and high-performance Brembo brakes, providing precise handling and stability. Its aggressive styling, single-sided swingarm, and aerodynamic bodywork make it instantly recognizable on both road and track. With its blend of speed, agility, and timeless design, the Ducati 916 remains a legendary choice for enthusiasts seeking a true race-inspired superbike experience.

Exterior

The exterior of this pre-owned Ducati 916 retains its iconic, aggressive styling that has made it a standout on both roads and tracks for decades. The paint still holds much of its glossy finish, accentuating the bike’s sharp lines, single-sided swingarm, and signature fairing design. As with most used superbikes, there are minor scratches on the bodywork from regular riding, but these are purely cosmetic and do not affect performance or handling. Overall, the body panels remain in good condition, making this 916 an appealing choice for riders who want a classic, race-inspired superbike with only light signs of wear.

FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

Engine & Performance

The Ducati 916 is powered by a 916cc V-twin engine with four valves per cylinder and desmodromic valve actuation. This liquid-cooled engine produces 114 bhp at 9,000 rpm and a peak torque of 90 Nm at 6,900 rpm, enabling a top speed of approximately 260 km/h (160 mph). It uses cam belts for precise valve timing, combining high-performance engineering with reliability to deliver an exhilarating riding experience.

Design & Aesthetics

Designed by the legendary Massimo Tambourine, the Ducati 916 is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful motorcycles ever created. Its unslung exhaust and twin Terminomic silencers add distinctive flair, while the ergonomics were carefully crafted to provide comfort and achieve a balanced 50/50 weight distribution for riders of various sizes.

Chassis & Suspension

The bike features a lightweight steel trellis frame that provides a strong and rigid structure. Its single-sided swingarm enhances both aesthetics and functionality, allowing faster wheel and tire changes—a signature Ducati design element. Upside-down Showa forks at the front, Ohlin's rear suspension, and Brembo brakes complete the package, giving the 916 precise handling, stability, and excellent stopping power.

Racing Heritage

The Ducati 916 was a dominant force on the track, winning multiple World Superbike Championships and earning a reputation as a successful racing machine. Its design also emphasized ease of maintenance for racing purposes, which was a key factor in its competitive success and enduring legacy in the sport bike world.
